          READ A PERSON

          When you try to understand someone's emotions or motives, roll+Power.

          • 10+: Ask MC 3 questions from list below
          • 7-9: Ask MC 1 question from list
          • 6-: MC tells you what they want you to think

          Questions: What are you really feeling? What do you want from me? What are you hiding? How could I get you to ___?

          TURN SOMEONE ON

          When you try to seduce, manipulate, or provoke someone, roll+Power.

          • 10+: They choose: do what you want, or MC gives them Condition
          • 7-9: Same, but you must promise something or give them String
          • 6-: They can give you Condition or String on you

          SHUT SOMEONE DOWN

          When you dismiss, embarrass, or physically hurt someone, roll+Power.

          • 10+: Choose 2 from list
          • 7-9: Choose 1 from list
          • 6-: They choose 1 against you

          Options: Gain String on them, hurt them (Condition), make them leave, they can't use moves on you this scene

          HOLD STEADY

          When you face fear, stress, or overwhelming emotion, roll+Power.

          • 10+: You're good, act with confidence
          • 7-9: MC chooses: Condition, flee, freeze up, or lash out
          • 6-: MC makes hard move

          LASH OUT PHYSICALLY

          When you attack someone with violence, roll+Power.

          • 10+: Inflict harm and choose 1
          • 7-9: Inflict harm but choose 1 for MC
          • 6-: You're outmatched, MC makes move

          Your choices: Extra harm, don't take harm back, gain String. MC choices: You take harm, Condition, lose String, terrible position

          When you flee danger or conflict, roll+Power.

          • 6-: MC makes move

          • 6-: Truth is worse than you imagined, take -1 ongoing until you process it
