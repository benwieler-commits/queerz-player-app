STRIKE A POSE

When you attempt to give yourself or your allies a positive status, or make an impression or influence others, roll 2d6 + Power.

10+: Choose 2 upgrades

7-9: Choose 1 upgrade, gain 1 Juice

6-: MC makes a move

Upgrades: More coverage, Impressive, Lasting, Contagious

SLAY

When you give your opponents a negative status or overcome their Ignorance Limit, roll 2d6 + Power.

10+: Choose 2 upgrades, gain 3 Juice

7-9: Choose 1 upgrade, gain 1 Juice

6-: MC makes a move

Upgrades: More coverage, Impressive, Lasting

GET A CLUE

When you investigate, research, or gather information, roll 2d6 + Power.

10+: Gain a Story Tag and ask 2 questions, gain 3 Juice

7-9: Ask 1 question or gain a Story Tag, gain 1 Juice

6-: MC makes a move

TALK IT OUT

When you de-escalate or negotiate in good faith, roll 2d6 + Power.

10+: Make progress or bond, gain 3 Juice

7-9: Succeed with cost, gain 1 Juice

6-: They put up walls

CARE

When you help someone with their emotional or physical needs, roll 2d6 + Power.

10+: Remove negative or give positive status, gain 3 Juice

7-9: As above but take status or mark Growth, gain 1 Juice

6-: Your care backfires

BE VULNERABLE

When you open up emotionally or share something difficult, roll 2d6 + Power.

10+: Mark Growth, choose 2 benefits, gain 3 Juice

7-9: Mark Growth, choose 1 benefit, gain 1 Juice

6-: Mark Growth but MC makes a move

Benefits: Gain support, clear status, earn trust, inspire others

RESIST

When you fight against a status being applied or try to reduce its tier, roll 2d6 + Power.

10+: Fully resist or reduce by 2 tiers, gain 3 Juice

7-9: Reduce status by 1 tier, gain 1 Juice

6-: Take the full status
